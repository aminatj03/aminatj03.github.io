Shipping Information

UK Delivery Info

Good news, as a customer of House of CB you're entitled to our standard free delivery service. Standard delivery operates weekdays Mon - Fri 8am to 7pm. To ensure you get all your goods before the weekend, please place your order by Tuesday to receive by Friday at the latest.

All UK orders take approx 2-3 working day, however if your item is located in a different warehouse it can take up to 6-7 working days.

Please note, we do not take any responsibility for delivery to shipping companies or freight forwarders, this will be at your own risk.

NEXT DAY DELIVERY

Need that item asap? All our items state whether they’re available for UK next working day delivery. If it’s a match, then next working day delivery needs to be placed by 4.00pm to be received the following day by 7.00pm latest.

Next day delivery costs 5.99GBP.

Next day delivery is only for the next working day, so if you’ve placed an order on Friday after 4.30pm or the weekend, then you’ll receive it on the following Tuesday.

Bank holidays and public holidays are for resting, so any next day deliveries placed on these days will be despatched on the next working day and delivered to the customer the following day.

Unfortunately, we don’t deliver to all postcodes. Click HERE to make sure you don’t miss out.

Please note, we do not take any responsibility for delivery to shipping companies or freight forwarders, this will be at your own risk.

SATURDAY DELIVERY

Saturday delivery is delivered on the first Saturday after the order is placed. Orders must be placed on or before Friday by 4.30pm to be delivered on Saturday.

Saturday delivery costs 10GBP.

If you’re nominated day is Saturday but you’ve ordered in the week, it’s cool. We hold on to your items and make sure it’s despatched on time so it’s delivered exactly when you want it.

Please note, we do not take any responsibility for delivery to shipping companies or freight forwarders, this will be at your own risk.

DELIVERY SERVICE

All our deliveries are handled by DPD or Parcelforce.

Reliability is key, which is why we use a signed-for delivery service.

Please make sure you or someone you know will be present either at a home or work address to accept your delivery. If no-one is in, we’ll make sure we leave a card so that a re-delivery can be arranged, or you can collect from the depot.

Please don’t refuse a delivery. Unfortunately, refused deliveries will incur a cost of the original delivery plus the return cost. Any refund made will be minus the total delivery and return cost in the case of refused deliveries.

Please note, we do not take any responsibility for delivery to shipping companies or freight forwarders, this will be at your own risk.

Returns and Exchanges

Guess what! We have return depots located in the UK, USA, Australia, Canada and Spain, making it easy to return to your items. The best part? Return addresses will be included in your order.

All orders placed now can be returned within 28 days of being received. This applies to online order and purchases made in our store.

Our stores will also be accepting return of online purchases so you can drop your return in store if that fits best. However, in concessions you will only be able to return orders purchased in that concession.

RETURNS

Sometimes the fit isn’t right, so here at House of CB we’re happy to accept returns on any garment that is unworn and still fitted with all security tags and labels. This applies to any item despatched to us within 28 days of receipt by you.

It’s important to note that shipping costs are not covered by House of CB when sent back, so we suggest using your local post office for the best service and tracking ID.

Luckily, our return instructions on the returns sheet enclosed with your delivery makes sending it back hassle free. Make sure you mail your returns back to us within 28 days of receipt.

Mislaid your returns sheet? Don’t panic. Just send us over an email at RETURNS@HOUSEOFCB.COM and we’ll be happy to help you out.

When filling out the sheet, please make sure you indicate if you would like a refund or an exchange

Some items are not eligible for a return or refund, but don’t worry. We make sure our designs clearly state this for you.

Payments will be credited back to the original purchasers credit card. Returns for refund are processed within 2-3 working days of receiving the item. Please note SALE items are refunded via Gift Cards.

Card refunds may take up to 10 business days for your bank to complete, depending on their processing time. This can vary between banks, so please speak to your bank for any more info regarding processing times as we are unable to amend this.

We’re happy to process store credit refunds and these will be displayed as a code in your email inbox. This will be made available immediately after 2-3 days of processing.

Credit notes can only be used in our online store.

We don't take any responsibility for lost parcels whilst being returned to us.

EXCHANGES

Sometimes the fit isn’t right, so here at House of CB we’re happy to accept exchanges on any garment that is unworn and still fitted with all security ribbons and labels. This applies to any item despatched to us within 28 days of receipt by you.

Exchanges take around 2-3 working days to process, plus the delivery time to your area.

It’s important to note that shipping costs are not covered by House of CB when sent back.

Luckily, our return instructions on the returns sheet enclosed with your delivery makes sending it back hassle free. Make sure you mail your returns back to us within 28 days of receipt and identify which item you would like replaced.

Mislaid your returns sheet? Don’t panic. Just send us over an email at RETURNS@HOUSEOFCB.COM and we’ll be happy to help you out.

Feel free to be picky. You can exchange your item for any size or item (permitted it’s in stock) and we send them back to you at no extra charge. We offer 2 exchanges for every item.

If you want to treat yourself even more and the replacement costs extra, a gift card will need to be purchased for the difference. Please let us know if you have made the purchase at RETURNS@HOUSEOFCB.COM

If the replacement is less expensive than the original, don’t worry. We’ll make sure the difference is refunded back to your original payment methods. Alternatively, you may refund the original item and then purchase the new, lower-priced item.

SALE ITEMS

We can only refund returned sale items by credit note for use on our online store.

3 for £160 deals are not returnable for exchange, refund or store credit.

£45 sale items are not returnable for exchange, refund or store credit.

5 for £100 promos are not returnable for exchange, refund or store credit.

LATE RETURNS

We’re unable to accept returns beyond the 28 days of your receipt and anything sent to us after 28 days may be returned to you.

CARRIERS TO RETURN

Use your local post office with a signed-for service and ask for a tracking number so you can follow your return. Don't use DHL or TNT to return your orders as these carriers will not deliver to us.

Our Policy

We take pride of our House of CB items, so anything returned should be new, unused and with all our security garment tags still attached. Returns that are damaged, soiled or altered may not be accepted and may be sent back to the customer.

If you’re returning something faulty, damaged or soiled please contact RETURNS@HOUSEOFCB.COM prior to sending any return.

Please note, we do not take any responsibility for delivery to shipping companies or freight forwarders, this will be at your own risk.

Insurance

House of CB insures each purchase during the time it’s in transit until it’s delivered to you. For all deliveries we require a signature, at which point responsibility for your purchased goods passes to you.

Not the receiver of the purchase? If you got your House of CB items as a gift or something similar, then the person who signs for the package confirms receipt and is accountable for the package.

Some Useful Tips

We’d hate for you to not receive your order. Please make sure that the shipping address is correct as we’re unable to redirect orders once they’re on their way to you.

Your security is important to us, so before we can dispatch your purchase we may need you to confirm your details with your card issuer. Delays aren’t fun for anyone so we’ll do our best to make it quick.

Our delivery time starts from the moment an order is accepted. This includes a 24 period where your items will be processed and dispatched by our warehouse. Please note this can take longer during our sale periods, when it may take up to 48 hours for shipments to be dispatched.

We’re all about good relationships and we work closely with our shipping partner to minimise the potential impact of custom delays on our international customers.

To minimise delays, we recommend using your work address as your delivery address.
